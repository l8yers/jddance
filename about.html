About JD Dance

Jennie Doolan Dance CIC was established in 2016. We are a local dance school in Broughton where Salford borders Manchester. Our aim is to provide a safe space in which children can gain skills, have fun and grow confidence.

JD DANCE is a family which builds confidence through high quality, affordable dance classes for children from 2–16 years old. All of our teachers are DBS checked, first aid trained and have safeguarding training regularly.
